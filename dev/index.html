Lamps

Bradleys Lamps® were born from the ideal that our kids should have the opportunity to have some fun as well as function in there rooms. After years of unsuccessfully looking for the ‘perfect lamp’ for my two young kids, Kobe and Tayla, I decided to reverse engineer the buying process and have them tell me what they wanted.
Today, Bradleys Lamps® are the best new kids lamps on the market. Built for safety, efficiency and fun these lamps will inspire playfulness in any kids room. With imaginative designs, colours characters Bradleys Lamps® allow perfect illumination. All our lamps are energy efficient and more importantly none have sharp edges or exposed bulbs.

Parcel & Letter Box

Bradleys Parcel and Letter Box® is set to take the parcel delivery market by storm. This unique delivery system enables all couriers with the opportunity of delivering parcels to a household in a secure and convenient way. Bradleys Parcel and Letter® box will also improve the online shopping experience for buyers, by providing peace of mind that there parcel will be waiting for them when they get home. No more lining up at Australia Post for a missed delivery.
Utilizing both modern design and practical functionality the Bradleys Parcel and Letter box® will soon be the norm at the front of every Australian home.

Bradleys International®

Bradleys International® is an Australian owned and operated wholesale business. Our mission statement, “creating innovative products that provide convenience and style”, promotes our business model, our commitment to quality and our current and future business direction. Bradleys International® head office is located in Tullamarine, Melbourne with an agent network spanning the entire country.

We have a small portfolio of products that are designed for functionality and to meet gaps in the current marketplace. Bradleys lamps® as well as Bradleys Parcel and Letter Box® are the two product ranges ready to hit the Australia market
